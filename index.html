About Me

Dr. Dimitrios Rallakis possesses a primary research interest and expertise in the exploration and characterization of energy reserves, specifically hydrocarbons, coal, and uranium ore. He has extensive experience in the fields of organic petrography, geochemistry, mineral petrography, and crystal chemistry.

Dr. Rallakis holds a doctoral degree (PhD) in Uranium Roll-Front Metallogenic Systems. He was formally engaged as a lecturer, delivering courses on Energy Resources and Coal Geology.

Currently, he works as a Characterizations Engineer, focusing on Electron Microscopy techniques (SEM-EDS, EPMA) and microXRF analytical instrumentation.
